Soul Filcher's Exploring Time
By Soul Filcher, 2020.
Downloaded from Steam Workshop.

This mod adds more light items like gas lanterns and torches. I may add other items
related to exploration in the future

Russian translation thanks to Mr.Hardcore on Steam.

-----ADDED ITEMS - MISC-------------------------------------------------------------

-Bottle With Gas:     Fill water or pop bottles with gas to refill a gas lantern.
-Flare:               Rare item that provides strong lighting for a short time.
-Gas Lantern:         Efficient when you're out of batteries.
-Torch:               Easy to make but not durable.
